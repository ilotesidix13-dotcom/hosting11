Our story

Founded by a carpenter and a designer

Meridian & Oak began in 2008 in a small Chicago workshop, built on a simple frustration: clients were forced to juggle a contractor, a furniture store, and a designer who never spoke to one another.

Today we are a fully licensed and insured U.S. company with an in-house design studio, a working cabinetry shop, and our own installation crews — serving homeowners across 48 states with one accountable team.

— Daniel & Mara, Founders
